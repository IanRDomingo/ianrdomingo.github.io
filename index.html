Research

My work is mostly about making training dynamics and representations better behaved, with mechanisms that have a “biological flavor” (local constraints, competition/sparsity, and balanced signal flow).

  • Neural Balancing (core): balancing input/output weight norms across units (neurons/channels/heads) to stabilize learning and improve performance.
  • Balancing in vision CNNs: experiments across model families (e.g., VGG-style and residual networks), plus careful multi-seed reproducibility runs.
  • Transformer head diversity + sparsity: PushApart (encourage diverse attention heads) and LWTA / k-WTA over heads (select top-k heads per input).
  • LLM memorization analysis: controlled experiments on smaller Llama models, scaling to larger variants when feasible.
  • Multimodal model behavior / safety: robustness, bias, and vulnerability analysis for models using text + audio/vision signals.
  • Medical imaging (MRI): generative modeling and translation (VAE, diffusion, and T1→T2), evaluated with metrics like SSIM/PSNR.
